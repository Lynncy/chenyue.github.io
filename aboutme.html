Yue Chen is a postdoctoral researcher working on human factors, ergonomics, and human-computer interaction. She has been working in the lab of Associate Professor Qin Gao since 2014. Her previous research focused on:

  • Investigating the user needs and motivations that drive users to use a certain technology
  • Designing, developing, and evaluating user interfaces
  • Measuring mental states such as emotion and attention

She is especially interested in improving the learning experience. Her Ph.D. dissertation focuses on the interactions in online learning and the design of tools to facilitate discussions during video lectures. She aims to engage people in lifelong learning. She is currently working on promoting older adults’ learning.
